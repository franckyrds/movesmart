🌍 Discover Around Dubai

Dubai is an excellent base for exploring the United Arab Emirates. Within less than two hours, discover magnificent mosques, mountains, nature reserves and landscapes completely different from Dubai's famous skyline.

👑 Abu Dhabi: Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque & Qasr Al Watan

Discover Abu Dhabi's highlights on a day trip from Dubai. Admire the magnificent Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, explore the stunning presidential palace of Qasr Al Watan, enjoy panoramic views from the Etihad Towers, stop for photos at the Louvre Abu Dhabi and drive along the capital's famous Corniche.

Book the Excursion →

🎨 Louvre Abu Dhabi

Designed by Jean Nouvel, the Louvre Abu Dhabi is as impressive for its spectacular floating dome as it is for its collections featuring works from around the world. A must-visit destination for lovers of art, history and contemporary architecture.

Book Tickets →

⛰️ Hatta

Escape the energy of Dubai and discover the spectacular landscapes of Hatta. Between rugged mountains, turquoise waters and traditional villages, this excursion also includes a kayaking experience in one of the most beautiful natural regions of the United Arab Emirates.

Book the Excursion →

🦩 Ras Al Khor

Just minutes from downtown Dubai, Ras Al Khor Wildlife Sanctuary offers a striking contrast to the city's skyscrapers. Observe hundreds of flamingos and many bird species in one of the most surprising natural areas in the Emirates.

🏜️ North & West Qatar

Discover Qatar's hidden treasures on a day trip from Doha. Explore the traditional port of Al Khor, visit the UNESCO-listed Al Zubarah Fort and admire the desert landscapes of the west coast, famous for their remarkable rock formations.

Why Is Dubai So Fascinating?

Dubai has become one of the world's most spectacular destinations in just a few decades. Between futuristic architecture, beaches, desert landscapes and premium experiences, the city offers a unique combination of modernity and lifestyle.

Downtown Dubai, Marina, Palm Jumeirah, Business Bay and JBR each offer their own atmosphere. Dubai attracts visitors for its rooftops and beach clubs as much as for its shopping, hotels and nightlife.

Thanks to its modern metro system, world-class infrastructure and countless attractions, Dubai is also one of the easiest cities to explore for a premium city break.

🚇 How Do You Get Around Dubai?

The Dubai Metro, taxis and apps such as Careem make it easy to travel between the city's main districts.

🌴 When Is the Best Time to Visit Dubai?

The ideal period is between November and April when temperatures are more comfortable for enjoying beaches and outdoor activities.

🏙️ What Are the Must-Do Experiences in Dubai?

Burj Khalifa, the desert, Dubai Marina, Palm Jumeirah, Dubai Mall and beach clubs are among the city's most iconic experiences.

Dubai Between Luxury, Desert and Futuristic Skylines

Between giant skyscrapers, modern rooftops, artificial beaches and a unique Middle Eastern atmosphere, Dubai delivers a spectacular urban experience built around lifestyle and escape.
