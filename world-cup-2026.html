🏨 Accommodation for the 2026 FIFA World Cup

Millions of fans are expected to travel across North America during the tournament. Booking accommodation early is highly recommended, especially in major host cities.

New York, Miami, Los Angeles, Dallas, Toronto, Vancouver and Mexico City are expected to experience particularly high demand during match days.

Choose accommodation close to stadiums, city centers or public transportation to make your World Cup experience as smooth as possible.
