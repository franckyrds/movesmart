🚋 Tram & transport

The Naolib network makes it easy to reach the city centre, the railway station, the Machines of the Isle, the historic districts and Nantes' main attractions.

🐘 Must-see places

The Machines of the Isle, Château des Ducs de Bretagne, Passage Pommeraye, Trentemoult and the Jardin des Plantes are among the city's essential sights.

⚽ Sport & events

FC Nantes, HBC Nantes, Hellfest, the Nantes Marathon and Le Voyage à Nantes bring the city to life throughout the year.

🌿 Nature & the Loire

With the banks of the Erdre, the Loire, urban parks and cycling routes, Nantes offers a unique balance between city life and nature.
