🌊 Mediterranean Atmosphere

Palma combines turquoise beaches, an elegant marina, a charming Old Town and stylish rooftops in a premium Mediterranean setting.

🏖️ Must-See Areas

La Seu Cathedral, Santa Catalina, Cala Major, Bellver Castle and the Paseo Marítimo are among Palma's most iconic places to explore.

🚍 Getting Around

Palma Airport (PMI) is just a few minutes from the city centre, while the EMT bus network provides easy access to beaches and the city's main districts.

☀️ Best Time to Visit

From April to October, Palma enjoys sunny weather, pleasant temperatures and spectacular sunsets over the Mediterranean Sea.
