The metro network provides quick access to the historic center, the airport, Piraeus and the city’s main tourist districts.

The Acropolis, the Parthenon, the Temple of Olympian Zeus, the Ancient Agora and the Panathenaic Stadium are among the city’s major sights.

Plaka, Monastiraki, Psyrri and the Athens Riviera offer a unique Mediterranean atmosphere.

Rooftops overlooking the Acropolis, hidden bars, traditional Greek tavernas and lively summer evenings bring the city to life.
