🚆 Transportation & Getting Around

The Metrorail, the free Metromover, Miami-Dade buses, taxis and Brightline make it easy to travel between Downtown, Brickell, Miami Beach and Miami International Airport.

🌴 Beaches & Must-See Attractions

South Beach, Ocean Drive, Key Biscayne, Bayside Marketplace and Wynwood are among Miami's most iconic locations, combining beaches, culture and a tropical atmosphere.

🎨 Neighborhoods & Culture

Wynwood, Little Havana, Brickell, Downtown Miami and the Design District offer completely different experiences, from street art and Cuban culture to shopping and modern architecture.

☀️ Experiences & Leisure

Biscayne Bay cruises, the Everglades, Miami Heat games, South Beach, the Florida Keys and tropical sunsets are among Miami's most unforgettable experiences.
