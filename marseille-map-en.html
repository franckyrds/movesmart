🚇 Metro & Tramway

The two metro lines and three tramway lines make it easy to reach the Old Port, Joliette, Castellane, Prado and Marseille's main neighborhoods.

⚓ Old Port & Le Panier

Marseille's historic heart brings together the Old Port, the colorful streets of Le Panier, lively terraces and the city's main monuments.

🌊 Corniche & Calanques

The Corniche Kennedy, Prado beaches and Calanques offer some of the most beautiful views of the Mediterranean, between turquoise waters and limestone cliffs.

⛪ Notre-Dame de la Garde

Nicknamed “La Bonne Mère”, the basilica overlooks Marseille and offers spectacular views over the Old Port, the Frioul Islands and the entire city.
